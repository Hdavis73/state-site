Texas

Texas; home of the cowboys, Tex-Mex, and good ol' southern hospitality. Where the weather's hot, the drinks are cold, and the state pride is unwavering. Here you'll find a Texas flag in every nook and cranny of the state, from the backyard of an old farmhouse to the parking lot of every major city's WhataBurger. Texas is especially known for being a state where "everything's bigger", and while this does ring true for cowboy hats and food portions, it also applies to the state's big open landscapes. The countryside in Texas is vast and diverse, with a view for almost everyone to enjoy.
